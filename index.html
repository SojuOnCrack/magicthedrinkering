🏆 Bracket Calculator
Official 5-bracket Commander system (Wizards of the Coast) — based on Game Changers list, combos, and deck-building rules
1
Exhibition
No Game Changers, no combos, no extra turns, no land denial. Play for fun and story.
2
Core
No Game Changers, no infinite combos. Sparse tutors and extra turns acceptable.
3
Upgraded
Up to 3 Game Changers. No early combos, no mass land denial, extra turns not chained.
4
Optimized
4+ Game Changers, cheap combos, mass land denial, or chained extra turns. Strongest decks.
5
cEDH
Competitive metagame mindset. Tournament structure, no pet cards, win at all costs.

B2 triggerNo Game Changers. Sparse tutors (1–4). Sparse extra turns (1–2). No infinite combos. Efficient ramp, removal, counterspells all fine.
